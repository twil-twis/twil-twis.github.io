Dictionary

Words in Vecinis have a primary meaning, and may also have several secondary meanings. The primary meaning is listed first, and is in bold.

Primary meanings and secondary meanings are important for grammatical reasons.

ankjot

  • An. N: woman, girl
  • Adj.: female

arjet

  • Verb: to arrest, to admonish
  • An. N: an alibi
  • In. N: a warrant, esp. as a piece of paper or document

arrib

  • In. N: a book; (old) a ledger
  • An. N: a story, information
  • Tu Ledjeqk̗ arribz̗ / Tu Ledjeqk̗ sr̂ikjeva arrbiz̗: the hand of fate (Ledjeq's ledger / Ledjeq's glass ledger)

ar̂o

  • Adj.: Hello! (Use with the copula on the person greeted: Tuš̗ ar̂ov̗!)

ax

  • Interjection: ugh, wow, damn
  • Can be used as a suffix:
    • After a: -x
    • Else: -ax

baced

  • An. N: a visionary
  • In. N: an extremist

bikot

  • An. N: a friend
  • Adj.: friendly
  • Verb: to unite

bjakâ

  • Adj.: black, dark

bod

  • An. N: body (of something living)
  • In. N: corpse; chassis

bon

  • In. N: beans, the seed of a bean plant
  • An. N: a bean plant

brunjots

  • In. N: armaments, weapons.
  • An. N: (coll.) the artificial weaponry used by the Vecin Seretsbon̗ark
  • Adj.: Armed, dangerous

-bezk

  • Suffix: using the noun non-physically
  • bezki̗, 'ski̗: because (of the proceeding phrase), due to, with

c

  • Used like "with" or "of" in certain phrases
  • "ra c kâr̂ûn" is the name for the letter r̂

ci

  • Verb: to see, to look

drâs

  • In. N: glass
  • Verb: to polish

dux

  • Art.: The animate singular definite article

ðen

  • Art.: The animate plural definite article

ðo

  • Adv.: But, however, although

e

  • Art.: Used to indicate dialogue, something spoken, or a quoted word.

en

  • Conjunction: and (links two nouns together)
  • See also: jen

errek

  • An. N: electricity
  • Note: this is the name for electricity in Kâdros. People do not know what "electricity" is, only "errek."

ezu

  • Verb: to want
  • Adv.: let's, indicates encouragement

giž

  • Verb: to give (Use with -stâ, -bezk, or ir̂e- as an adverb; the object of the sentence is emphasized)

inum

  • Adj.: none of

imbz

  • In. N: clouds
  • An. N: snow

j'an

  • Adv.: converts a sentence into a yes-or-no question

jbi

  • Adj.: good

je

  • Interjection: yes
  • Adv.: used to emphasize a word, like "did" in "He did say..."

jen

  • Conjuction: and (links two phrases together)
  • See also: en

jero

  • Adj.: yellow

jtua

  • Verb: (trans.) to steal; (intr.) to run away

karon

  • Adj.: pointy, sharp (not along a sharp edge)
  • An. N: a point (physical or rhetoric)

karrik

  • An. N: fire; (in artifice) a unit of energy
  • In. N: (old) heat; (in artifice) energy source like a boiler

karrikc

  • In. N: warmth
  • An. N: heat
  • Adj.: hot

kats

  • An. N: a cat
  • Adj.: tricky; enjoys destruction

kav

  • Verb: to walk, to go

kâr̂ûn

  • In. N: the symbol "^" as in â, r̂, and û
  • See also: karon

kâv

  • Verb: to say, to talk about
  • An. N: speech, words
  • kâv šoa: to express oneself (kâv triki šoa: express one's happiness)

kâvis

  • An. N: language

ke

  • Verb: to do

kirrim

  • In. N: ice

kirrimc

  • In. N: cool, chill, coldness
  • An. N: freezing coldness
  • Adj.: cold, chilly

kjot

  • An. N: human, person
  • e'kjot: humanity, world

kjoð

  • Adj.: (vulgar) awful, shit, bad at what it does

korrek

  • In. N: brother, partner, colleague
  • An. N: brotherhood, partnership.

ke-

  • Prefix: Turns words without a defined verbal meaning into verbs.

litj

  • Adj.: small
  • Verb: to be less than (with the quality used as an adverb)

'la

  • Art.: The inanimate definite article

'lo

  • Art.: The inanimate indefinite article
  • When proceeding a vowel, becomes arr'- ('lo imbz -> arr'imbz)

mako

  • Adj.: big

max

  • An. N: epitome, best example, exemplary
  • Adv: very, good at what it does

maxn

  • Adv.: -est (biggest: maxni̗ mako)

numur

  • In. N: number
  • Number: unspecified amount

-ne

  • Suffix: something (usually inanimate) that performs the word automatically
  • Contrast with -re:
    • mor̂ûtne: something that kills automatically (a trap)
    • mor̂ûtre: something that helps the wielder kill (a weapon)

or

  • Adv.: pre-, in advance (like preorganized, preset, premeditated)

organiz

  • Verb: to organize, to clean up
  • An. N: a setup, a plan
  • In. N: a manual or list of instructions

pacon

  • In. N: a castle, a tower

pajbi

  • In. N: emotions, spirits; goodwill
  • An. N: the feeling of nervousness when doing something brave
  • T'Uk̗ pajbiz̗ bezki̗: Unfortunately, sorry (lit. "Because of your goodwill")

paju

  • An. N: a willow tree
  • In. N: a seed or cutting of a willow tree

paž

  • Adj.: Fancy
  • Verb: to comfort

pi

  • In. N: Here (does not take tûp)
  • (Used in pi-go statements)

pjasek

  • In. N: sand

pûs

  • Conjunction: plus

qe-

  • Prefix: Indicates that a word with a defined verbal meaning but that is not a verb by default is a verb

raxurrik

  • Adj: quiet, calm, placid; centered, meditative
  • Verb: to align or center (physically)

ri

  • Art.: The animate singular indefinite article

rin

  • Art.: The animate plural indefinite article

rupjš

  • An. N: peasant, farmer
  • Adj.: rustic; backwater

-re

  • Suffix: something inanimate that helps you perform the word

sapfunt

  • An. N: A scientist, a professor

savu

  • Verb: to believe, to think
  • An. N: idea
  • In. N: evidence, data

savua

  • Verb: to know
  • tužoi̗ savua: to remember, to recall

serets

  • Verb: to force, to enforce, to make someone do something
  • Adj.: resolute, steadfast
  • Adv.: must, are required to
  • seretsbo/ma: policeman, guard

sivjer

  • In. N: the metal silver
  • An. N: morals, rights
  • Adv.: ought to, is one's duty to
  • Adj.: true

sr̂ikje

  • In. N: glass (archaic; now implies it's of low quality)
  • Verb: to shatter; (informal) to shake it up or make a change

stâ

  • Verb: (transitive) to be at a place (permanantly); (intransitive) there exists, there are
  • qestâ: to be useful

-stâ

  • Suffix: using the noun physically

sti

  • Verb: to be at a place (temporarily); to arrive
  • Suffix: turns a noun into an adverb indicating where the verb happened

šoa

  • An. N: a demonstration, evidence, a mnemonic, something that makes it easier to understand
  • Verb: to understand

teik

  • Verb: to pick up and hold
  • Adj.: cares about

tejk

  • Verb: to select
  • Adv.: can, is able to; it's possible, maybe (usually sarcastic)

triki

  • Adj.: happy

Tu

  • An. N: You (formal)
  • Verb: to have
  • (Used to indicate possession)

tužo

  • Number: 2
  • Adv.: again

tûp

  • An. N: type, kind, sort
  • In. N: label, marker, indicator; (in Vecinis grammar) one of the affixes indicating parts of speech (-š̗, -k̗, -z̗, -q̗, -i̗, -va, qe-/ke-)

un

  • Number: 1
  • Adv.: same

ûn

  • Adj.: only, just

Ût

  • In. N: it, 3rd person inanimate pronoun
  • An. N: Used when a subject is required for grammatical reasons (like "It rained"); an unimportant 3rd party (like "they" in Strannic "They say that...")

viga

  • In. N: mark, blemish, imperfect part

Vecin

  • Adj: of Vecinâ
  • Vecinâ: the country
  • Vecinis: the language
  • Vecinkjot/Vecinikjot: A person (Vecinkjot in the North, Vecinikjot in the South)

vejksm

  • An. N: luck
  • In. N: something that gives an (unfair) advantage

vest

  • In. N: letter, character

vzixag

  • In. N: a well, a spring
  • An. N: a mine
  • Verb: (trans.) to draw from the earth; (intrans.) to dowse

xamet

  • An. N: metal, esp. when suitable for artificing
  • In. N: dross, slag
  • karrikz̗va ri xamet: the act of "burning the midnight oil" or "running on fumes" (ie, if your artifice is running on the heat in the metal that makes it up and not an actual energy source)

xat

  • An. N: heart, core,
  • In. N: essence of (esp. in chemistry), also as a suffix

xâv

  • Number: half

xes

  • Adv.: why?

zmouxi

  • Verb: to fuck (vulgar, obviously) (maybe I should leave this one out)

zmoxi

  • Verb: to like

žen

  • Number: several (usually 4-8)
